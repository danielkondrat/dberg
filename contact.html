Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

For weddings, I recommend booking 6-12 months in advance, especially for peak season (May-October). Portrait sessions can typically be scheduled 2-4 weeks out, though I sometimes have last-minute availability.

Do you travel for sessions?

Absolutely! I'm based in New York but love traveling for sessions. I've photographed weddings and events across the country. Travel fees may apply depending on location and duration.

How long until I receive my photos?

Wedding galleries are delivered within 6-8 weeks, portrait sessions within 2-3 weeks. You'll receive a sneak peek within 48 hours for weddings and 24 hours for other sessions.

What's included in your packages?

All packages include professional editing, high-resolution digital gallery, and print release. Wedding packages also include engagement session options and additional coverage hours. Specific details vary by package.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes! I understand that photography is an investment. I offer flexible payment plans with a 50% deposit to secure your date and the remainder due before your session or wedding day.

What happens if it rains on my wedding day?

Weather never stops a great photo! I come prepared with backup plans and equipment to handle any weather condition. Some of my favorite shots have been taken in unexpected weather.
