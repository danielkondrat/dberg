Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

Preferably 3-5 weeks in advance.

Do you travel for sessions?

Yes; however, locations over 35 miles from Niles, MI 49120 will require a mandatory travel fee.

How long until I receive my photos?

You can expect your photos within 10 business days of the shoot.

What happens if I need to cancel?

As outlined in our contract, The Photo / Video Session can be rescheduled within a 48 hour notice w/o being charged. If the photoshoot is cancelled within a 7 day time window your 50% deposit is FORFEITED.

How much does a shoot cost?

Every vision is different, and shoots are always customized. We have base prices for what you may want, but please note that we don't go entirely off of these prices, as each vision is different.
