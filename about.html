About The Owner

My Journey in Photography

I have lots of passions, and each one has shaped me in different ways. Flying and working towards a career in aviation has given me a sense of freedom and perspective — there’s nothing quite like seeing the world from above and realizing how vast and beautiful it is. Cooking has become a creative outlet for me, where I can experiment, unwind, and share something meaningful and tasty, with friends and family. Airsoft is another passion that combines strategy, teamwork, and adrenaline, teaching me discipline while also being a fun way to stay active. And of course, traveling is a passion that ties it all together. Exploring new places, meeting new people, and experiencing different cultures has not only enriched my life but also inspired much of my photography and videography.

These passions may seem varied, but they all connect back to creativity, exploration, and storytelling — the same qualities that drive my work behind the camera.

My journey with photography began at the age of 14, when a simple curiosity grew into a life-changing passion. At events, I would often notice photographers with their professional cameras. I eventually decided to ask if I could try taking a picture. Out of generosity, a few of them handed me their cameras, giving me the chance to experience firsthand the art of capturing a moment. That small act of kindness sparked something in me—an excitement and fascination that quickly developed into a lifelong love for both photography and videography.

Since then, photography has become much more than just a hobby; it has grown into a successful business. Over the years, I’ve been trusted by many clients to capture their most meaningful moments, from senior portraits and family sessions to professional headshots and corporate office advertising.

Each opportunity has pushed me to refine my skills, expand my creativity, and deliver work that not only meets expectations but exceeds them.

Photography & Videography has also opened doors I never could have imagined. My work has taken me from documenting special events and milestones to unique opportunities such as contributing to a movie production and regularly capturing life at Andrews Academy. These experiences have shaped my perspective, broadened my skill set, and deepened my appreciation for the power of storytelling through imagery and video.

While my journey started at a young age, I’ve always believed that age does not define the quality or professionalism of one’s work. Instead, I let my dedication, creativity, and results speak for themselves. I continually push myself to learn, grow, and embrace new challenges—because each project is an opportunity to become better at what I love.

Whether it’s portraits, events, or creative ventures, my mission remains the same: to capture life in its truest form, with authenticity, artistry, and heart. Every photo is more than just an image—it’s a story, a memory, and a piece of life preserved for years to come.

My Photography Philosophy

"I believe photography and videography are more than just images on a screen or paper—they are powerful tools for storytelling. Every person, every family, and every business has a story worth sharing, and my goal is to capture it with authenticity and creativity. For me, it’s not about staging perfection, but about preserving genuine moments: the laughter, the emotion, the details that make each story unique. Whether it’s a senior stepping confidently into the next chapter, a family cherishing time together, or a business showcasing its vision, I approach every project with care, artistry, and respect for the people behind the lens. My philosophy is simple: create work that is both beautiful and meaningful. I want my photos and videos to do more than just document a moment—I want them to spark memories, stir emotion, and stand as timeless reminders of life’s most important chapters."
— Dylan Bergmann Owner of Bergmann Productions
